The Cosmos Troupe, or Soragumi, is the youngest troupe of the Revue, formed in 1998. It is represented by the colour purple. The troupe's Top Stars are changing this summer due to the retirement of Makaze Suzuho and Jun Hana, pictured right. The new Top Stars will be:

Serika Toa, Top Otokoyaku, and

Haruno Sakura, Top Musumeyaku.

My first Takarazuka show was Soragumi's 'Elizabeth' (2016) starring Asaka Manato as Der Tod and Misaki Rion as Empress Elizabeth of Austria. My favourite show from the troupe is Top Hat (2015), starring Manato as Jerry Travers and Rion as Dale Tremont.
