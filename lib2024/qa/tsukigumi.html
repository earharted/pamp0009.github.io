The Moon Troupe, or Tsukigumi, is one of the original troupes of the Revue. It is represented by the colour yellow. The troupe's current Top Stars are

Tsukishiro Kanato, Top Otokoyaku, and

Umino Mitsuki, Top Musumeyaku.

My personal favourite show from Moon Troupe is Me and My Girl (2013), starring Ryuu Masaki as Bill Snibson and Manaki Reika as Sally Smith.
