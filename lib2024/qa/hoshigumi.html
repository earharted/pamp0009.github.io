Star Troupe

The Star Troupe, or Hoshigumi, is the fourth troupe of the Revue, formed in 1933. It is represented by the colour blue. The troupe's current Top Stars are

Makoto Rei, Top Otokoyaku, and

Maisora Hitomi, Top Musumeyaku.

My personal favourite show from Star Troupe is A Second Fortuitous Meeting-Next Generation: Midnight Girlfriend- (2022), pictured to the right. It stars Makoto Rei as Luce and Maisora Hitomi as Angelique.
