The Flower Troupe, or Hanagumi, is one of the two original troupes in the Revue. It is represented by the colour pink. The troupe's current Top Stars are

Yuzuka Rei, Top Otokoyaku, and

Hoshikaze Madoka, Top Musumeyaku.

My personal favourite show from Flower Troupe is 'Nice Work If You Can Get It!' from 2021, starring Yuzuka Rei as Jimmy Winter and Hana Yuuki as Billie Bendix.
