Princess Mononoke

My Favourite Film

One of my favourite films of all time is Princess Mononoke (Mononoke Hime), an animated feature from Studio Ghibli released in 1997.

Plot

Ashitaka, prince of the Emishi people, is cursed by a demonic beast, forcing him to leave his home to find a way to cleanse himself.
He travels through the forest and ends up in Iron Town, a mining settlement governed by Lady Eboshi. Ashitaka finds himself caught
in a fight between Eboshi's people and the forest's inhabitants, led by Princess San, the Mononoke Hime(spirit princess).

Top Cast(English Release)

Billy Crudup as Ashitaka, the Emishi prince

Claire Danes as San, the forest princess

Minnie Driver as Lady Eboshi, leader of the humans

Billy Bob Thornton as Jigo, a suspicious monk

Gillian Anderson as Moro, leader of the wolves and San's mother

My Favourite Line

"Wanting everything between Heaven and Hell is the human condition." -- Jigo

Ratings

IMDB - 8.4 stars out of 10

Rotten Tomatoes - 93%(critics) 94%(audience)

Princess Mononoke has won 10 film awards, including the 1998 Picture of the Year award at the Japan Academy Awards.
